About Relational Life Texas

Founded in 2010, Relational Life Texas emerged from a vision to transform couples therapy in San Antonio. Our founder, Dr. Sarah Martinez, recognized the need for a more comprehensive, evidence-based approach to relationship healing after 15 years of clinical practice.

Our mission is to provide couples with the tools, support, and guidance they need to build stronger, more fulfilling relationships. We believe in the power of connection and are committed to helping couples rediscover the joy and intimacy that brought them together.

Frequently Asked Questions

What can we expect in our first session?

Your initial 90-minute consultation includes a comprehensive assessment of your relationship dynamics, goal setting, and the development of a customized treatment plan. We'll address immediate concerns and establish a framework for moving forward.

How long does couples therapy typically take?

Most couples see significant improvement within 12-15 sessions, though this varies based on your specific situation and goals. We'll regularly assess progress and adjust our approach as needed.

What if my partner is reluctant to attend therapy?

This is common, and we offer individual consultations to help address concerns and build comfort with the therapy process. We can also provide resources and strategies for discussing therapy with a reluctant partner.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans, HSA/FSA accounts, and all major credit cards. We also offer sliding scale fees and payment plans for qualifying couples to ensure therapy is accessible.

Is online therapy available?

Yes, we offer secure video sessions using HIPAA-compliant platforms. Online therapy provides the same evidence-based treatment with added convenience and flexibility for busy couples.
