Frequently Asked Questions

How long are therapy sessions?

Most couples therapy sessions are 50-90 minutes long, depending on your needs and the treatment plan we develop together.

How many sessions will we need?

The number of sessions varies for each couple. We'll assess your situation and create a customized treatment plan during your initial consultation.
